History

Born in national interest in response to 2020's COVID19 pandemic, Swasth is an alliance of over 150 healthcare organisations.

Empowered by wise partnerships in the public, private, non-profit and academic sectors, Swasth has been instrumental in providing relief on a national scale during the crisis. Current efforts are focussed on building a Digitised Health Benefits Ecosystem as a first step to Universal Health Coverage.

This work holds the beginnings of an immensely impactful inter-operable, health benefits network covering the continuum of care
